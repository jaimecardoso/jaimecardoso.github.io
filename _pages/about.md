I am a [Full Professor](https://sigarra.up.pt/feup/en/FUNC_GERAL.FORMVIEW?p_codigo=367235) at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (DEEC), [Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP)](http://www.fe.up.pt/), Portugal.

I am also a researcher at [INESC TEC](http://www.inesctec.pt/) (Centre for Telecommunications and Multimedia), where I co-founded:

- the Breast Research Group  
- the [Visual Computing and Machine Intelligence (VCMI) Group](http://vcmi.inesctec.pt/)  

My research focuses on **computer vision**, **pattern recognition**, and **medical decision support systems**.

If you are interested in pursuing a PhD in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, or Medical Decision Support Systems, feel free to contact me.

Learn [here](https://study-research.pt/) why you should study and do research in Portugal.
